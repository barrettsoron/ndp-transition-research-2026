After Avi Lewis was selected as the new leader of the NDP, I gave a long interview with a journalist assessing his strengths and weaknesses. That interview was then posted onto a left-wing online group. I found it, and readied myself for the inevitable avalanche of criticism and insults.

To my surprise, the comments were generally positive, with one praising me for being reasonable and fair in my assessment of Lewis.
