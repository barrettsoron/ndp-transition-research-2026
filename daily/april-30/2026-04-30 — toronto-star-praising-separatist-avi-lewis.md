Toronto Star political opinion column criticizing Avi Lewis's response to MP Alexandre Boulerice's move from the federal NDP to Québec Solidaire. The column argues that Lewis's framing of Boulerice's move as a continuation of shared values — rather than a break with the NDP's federalist position — speaks to deeper questions about the party's stance on Quebec sovereignty under its new leader.
